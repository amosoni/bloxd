How to Go to the Nether in Bloxd

Everything you need to build a portal, travel safely, and what to do if Nether is disabled on your server.

Play Bloxd Now Back to Game

Portal Recipe & Checklist

  • 10–14 obsidian, flint & steel, extra blocks, food, and torches.
  • Bring gold armor to reduce piglin aggro in some server configs.
  • Protect your Nether spawn with blocks immediately after entry.

If Nether Is Disabled

  • Use server hubs with Nether-themed worlds or shops for resources.
  • Trade with players for blaze rods, quartz, and nether bricks.
  • Join a server listed on our Servers page that enables portals.
