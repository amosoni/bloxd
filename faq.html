Common Questions

How do I play Bloxd?

Simply visit bloxd.io in your web browser. No downloads or installations required! The game loads directly in your browser and you can start playing immediately. Make sure you have a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and a stable internet connection. The game typically takes 10-30 seconds to load depending on your connection speed.

What are the basic controls?

Movement: WASD keys to move, Space to jump, Shift to sneak
Actions: Left click to break blocks, Right click to place blocks
Inventory: E to open inventory, Q to drop items
View: Mouse to look around, F5 to change perspective

What game modes are available?

Bloxd offers several game modes:
• Survival: Gather resources, build, and survive
• Creative: Unlimited resources for building
• PvP: Player vs player combat
• Adventure: Explore and complete objectives
• Mini-games: Various fun challenges

What are the system requirements?

Minimum Requirements:
• Modern web browser (Chrome 80+, Firefox 75+, Safari 13+, Edge 80+)
• 4GB RAM
• Stable internet connection
• Graphics card with WebGL support

Recommended:
• 8GB RAM or more
• High-speed internet connection
• Dedicated graphics card

The game is laggy, what can I do?

Try these solutions to reduce lag:
• Close other browser tabs and applications
• Lower graphics settings in the game
• Use a wired internet connection
• Try a different browser
• Clear browser cache and cookies
• Restart your browser
• Check if your internet connection is stable

Why won't the game load?

Common causes and solutions:
• Browser Issues: Try updating your browser or using a different one
• Internet Connection: Check your internet connection
• Firewall/Antivirus: Temporarily disable to test
• Browser Extensions: Try disabling extensions
• Cache: Clear browser cache and cookies
• Device: Restart your device

How do I join multiplayer servers?

To join multiplayer servers:
1. Visit bloxd.io
2. Click on "Multiplayer" or "Servers"
3. Browse available servers
4. Click on a server to join
5. Wait for the server to load
6. Start playing with other players!

Can I play with friends?

Yes! You can play with friends by:
• Joining the same server at the same time
• Sharing server information with friends
• Using the friend system (if available)
• Coordinating through Discord or other communication platforms
• Creating private servers (if you have the option)

What should I do if someone is griefing?

If you encounter griefing:
• Don't retaliate: This can make the situation worse
• Report the player: Use the server's report system
• Contact moderators: Reach out to server staff
• Document evidence: Take screenshots or recordings
• Switch servers: Find a better community
• Block the player: If the option is available

Do I need an account to play?

No, you don't need an account to play Bloxd! The game is completely free and accessible to anyone with a web browser. However, creating an account can provide benefits like: • Saving your progress • Customizing your character • Accessing additional features • Participating in community events

How do I create an account?

To create an account:
1. Visit bloxd.io
2. Look for "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
3. Choose a username and password
4. Verify your email (if required)
5. Start playing with your new account!

Note: Account creation is optional and not required to play the game.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

If you forgot your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password
6. Log in with your new password

If you're still having trouble, contact support for assistance.
