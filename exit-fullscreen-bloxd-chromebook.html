Exit Full Screen in Bloxd (Chromebook)

Use the dedicated key or browser toggle—here are the fastest methods.

Back to Game

Quick Methods

  • Press the Full Screen key (typically F4 icon) once.
  • Press F11 to exit Chrome's fullscreen mode.
  • Press Esc to close in-game overlays.
  • Move cursor to the top edge and click the square icon in the toolbar.

Still stuck? Restart the tab, or open chrome://settings → Site Settings → Fullscreen and revoke permission.
