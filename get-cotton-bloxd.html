How to Get Cotton in Bloxd

Find it fast, then scale your farm for steady income or crafting.

Back to Game

Best Sources

  • Meadow & River edges: run a loop and mark spawn spots with torches.
  • Server shops: use /shop or market warps; buy in bulk during off-peak hours.
  • Trading: exchange extra stone/wood for cotton with builders.

Farming Tips

  • Plant in 9×9 plots with water in the center for efficient harvests.
  • Use bone meal (if enabled). Replant immediately to keep the loop going.
  • Store harvests near spawn routes for fast selling.
