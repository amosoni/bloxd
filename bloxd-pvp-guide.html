Advanced Combat Techniques

Strafing & Dodging

Master the art of evasive movement to avoid enemy attacks while maintaining your own offensive pressure.

  1. Move unpredictably while attacking
  2. Use A and D keys to strafe
  3. Jump to avoid low attacks
  4. Change direction suddenly
Weapon Switching

Learn to switch between weapons quickly to adapt to different combat situations.

  1. Use number keys for quick switching
  2. Switch based on distance
  3. Keep your best weapon ready
  4. Practice switching under pressure
Cover Usage

Use the environment to your advantage by taking cover and planning your attacks.

  1. Identify cover points before fighting
  2. Use cover to reload safely
  3. Peek around corners carefully
  4. Don't get trapped in cover
Team Coordination

Work with teammates to create tactical advantages and overwhelm opponents.

  1. Communicate with your team
  2. Coordinate attacks and defenses
  3. Cover each other's weaknesses
  4. Share resources and information

Pro PvP Tips

  • Practice Regularly: Consistent practice improves your skills and reaction time
  • Learn from Defeats: Analyze your losses to identify weaknesses and improve
  • Watch Top Players: Study the techniques of experienced players
  • Master One Weapon: Become expert with one weapon before learning others
  • Stay Calm: Panic leads to mistakes - maintain composure in battle
  • Use Sound: Listen for enemy footsteps and weapon sounds
  • Map Knowledge: Learn the layout of maps to use them to your advantage
  • Adapt Quickly: Change your strategy based on the situation
