How to Get Under Bedrock in Bloxd.io (Safely)

Back to Game

Before You Try

  • Check /rules. Many servers forbid void travel.
  • Always carry ladders, water bucket, and pearls for escape.
  • Never trap other players—this can be bannable griefing.

Allowed Routes (If Server Permits)

  • Piston/TNT open a single bedrock block, then staircase down and secure access.
  • Use portals to return; place signs with coordinates at the entry point.
  • Build safe paths and mark void holes clearly.
