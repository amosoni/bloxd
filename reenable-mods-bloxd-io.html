Checklist Before You Try

  • Read /rules or server website—some allow only cosmetic or performance mods.
  • Avoid automation/macros. Anything that plays for you risks ban.
  • Prefer server-approved resource/texture packs over script injectors.

Safer Alternatives

  • Use in-game performance options and lower render distance.
  • Ask staff for a whitelist of allowed client tweaks.
  • Join a mod‑friendly world on our Servers list.

When in doubt, play vanilla. Your account safety is worth more than a mod.
