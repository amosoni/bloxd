How to Make Your Dog Sit in Bloxd.io

Back to Game

Commands

  • Look at your pet and press Interact (default: Right‑Click or E) to toggle sit/stand.
  • Use /pet sit or /pet follow if the server provides pet commands.
  • Leads can force stay when tied to a fence.

If It Doesn't Work

  • Pets may be disabled on competitive servers—check /rules.
  • Relog to resync. Make sure you own the pet (tamed by you).
  • Ask staff if a protection plugin blocks interactions in that region.
